Programming Without and With AI

With AI Without AI
1- A computer program with AI can answer the generic questions it is meant to solve. 1- A computer program without AI can answer the specific questions it is meant to solve.
2- AI programs can absorb new modifications by putting highly independent pieces of information together. Hence you can modify even a minute piece of information of program without affecting its structure. 2- Modification in the program leads to change in its structure.
3- Quick and Easy program modification. 3- Modification is not quick and easy. It may lead to affecting the program adversely.
